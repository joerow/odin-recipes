How to make a Greek Salad

To make this simple salad all you need to do is chop up an assortment of fresh Mediterranean veggies and some olives, add a simple dressing and some feta and youâ€™ll have a delicious salad everyone will love in no time!

Ingredients

  • Roma Tomatoes (grape tomatoes are delicious too)
  • Cucumbers
  • Red Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Black Olives
  • Avocado
  • Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop all of the ingredients
  2. Google a Greek salad dressing recipe and prepare it
  3. Add your ingredients to a bowl
  4. Add the dressing to taste
  5. Enjoy!
